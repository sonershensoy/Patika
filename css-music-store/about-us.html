About Us

Mission

To provide traditional and unique instruments, related supplies, music education, and music promotion/advocacy with an emphasis on acoustic/folk genres

Vission

To become a destination hub for music enthusiasts and inspire greater participation in musical arts at all levels. Our vision is to provide opportunity and cultivate experiences through building folk instruments, learning to play various instruments, and developing appreciation for musical performance on a local, regional, and national level.
